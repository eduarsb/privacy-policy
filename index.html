Privacy Policy

edsertor — Last updated: June 17, 2026

Introduction

This privacy policy applies to mobile applications published by edsertor on the Google Play Store. We are committed to protecting your privacy. Our apps are designed with a local-first approach: your data stays on your device.

Data Collection

Our apps do not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Track your location
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Send data to external servers
  • Require user accounts or login

All data you create within the app (tasks, notes, settings, etc.) is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

Third-Party Services

Our apps do not integrate third-party services that collect user data. No advertising SDKs, analytics platforms, or social media trackers are included.

Permissions

Some apps may request device permissions (such as notifications or file access) to provide core functionality. These permissions are used solely for the intended feature and no data is collected or shared through them.

Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, its security is managed by your device's built-in protections (screen lock, encryption, etc.). If you uninstall an app, all associated data is permanently deleted.

Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
